About Suchita

I am a former management student, turned UX professional. After graduating from my college, I decided to go for a field which suits my personality.
UX design appealed to me because all my soft skills which I learned during my management course fitted so perfectly. During my college, I went through a lot of research and thesis which worked as a cherry on the top while learning UX principles and applying it on my projects.
